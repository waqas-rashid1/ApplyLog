What is Elevatr?

Elevatr is your personal assistant in the job search journey. It helps you track every application — whether it's from LinkedIn, Indeed, Rozee, or any other platform — and manage your job progress efficiently.

  • • Add applications quickly and easily
  • • Monitor statuses at a glance
  • • Stay on top of follow-ups
  • • Your data stays private and secure

Designed to simplify tracking, reduce stress, and increase your chances of success.
